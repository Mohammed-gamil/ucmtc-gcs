Launch Mission Simulator

You are about to spawn two terminal windows on the local host machine:

  • Terminal 1: Sources ROS2 Humble, colcon-builds rover_core, launches the rover bringup package.
  • Terminal 2: Echoes /rover/commands/motor to monitor live DDS telemetry dispatch.

Ensure you are in a graphical desktop session (GNOME/X11) with terminal support.
